Generate your IndexNow key with:  openssl rand -hex 16

Then:
  1. Set INDEXNOW_KEY=<that key> in your environment.
  2. Rename this file to <that key>.txt and replace the contents with only the key.
  3. Delete this notice file.

Engines will fetch https://yoursite.com/<key>.txt to verify ownership before
trusting your /api/indexnow pings.
